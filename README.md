In the era of "AI Slop"—where AI code assistants often produce messy, unstructured, and technically indebted code—**AI-Enderun** stands as a beacon of discipline. Inspired by the elite **Enderun** school of the Ottoman Empire, this framework treats AI agents not as simple chat-bots, but as high-ranking **Senior Engineers** who must follow a strict **Constitution (Gemini.md)** and a **Phase-Based Execution** model.
